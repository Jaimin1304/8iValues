What are the eight values? 这八种价值观是什么？

There are four independent axes - Economic, Diplomatic, Civil, and Society - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are: 分为四根独立的政治轴：经济 (Economic)、外交 (Diplomatic)、政治 (Civil) 和社会 (Society)，每根轴分配了两个相反的价值观：

EQUALITY

Those with higher Equality scores believe the economy should distribute value evenly among the populace. They tend to support progressive tax codes, social programs, and at high values, socialism. 在平等维度得分较高的人认为经济应在民众中均匀分配价值。他们倾向于支持累进税制、社会福利项目，在极端情况下支持社会主义。

ECONOMIC

MARKETS

Those with higher Market scores believe the economy should be focused on rapid growth. They tend to support lower taxes, privatization, deregulation, and at high values, laissez-faire capitalism. 在市场维度得分较高的人认为经济应注重快速增长。他们倾向于支持低税收、私有化、放松管制，在极端情况下支持自由放任资本主义。

NATION

Those with higher Nation scores are patriotic and nationalist. They often believe in an aggressive foreign policy, valuing the military, strength, sovereignty, and at high values, territorial expansion. 在国家维度得分较高的人具有爱国主义和民族主义倾向。他们通常主张强硬的外交政策，重视军事力量、国家主权，在极端情况下支持领土扩张。

DIPLOMATIC

GLOBE

Those with higher Globe scores are cosmopolitan and globalist. They often believe in a peaceful foreign policy, emphasizing diplomacy, cooperation, integration, and at high values, a world government. 在全球维度得分较高的人具有世界主义和全球化倾向。他们通常主张和平外交政策，强调外交合作、国际一体化，在极端情况下支持世界政府。

LIBERTY

Those with higher Liberty scores believe in strong civil liberties. They tend to support democracy and oppose state intervention in personal lives. Note that this refers to civil liberties, not economic liberties. 在自由维度得分较高的人信奉公民自由。他们倾向于支持民主制度，反对国家干预个人生活。请注意，这里指的是公民自由，而非经济自由。

CIVIL

AUTHORITY

Those with higher Authority scores believe in strong state power. They tend to support state intervention in personal lives, government surveillance, and at high values, censorship or autocracy. 在权威维度得分较高的人信奉强大的国家权力。他们倾向于支持国家干预个人生活、政府监控，在极端情况下支持审查制度或专制统治。

TRADITION

Those with higher Tradition scores believe in traditional values and strict adherence to a moral code. Though not always, they are usually religious, and support the status quo or the status quo ante. 在传统维度得分较高的人信奉传统价值观，严格遵守道德准则。他们通常（但不总是）有宗教信仰，支持维持现状或回归过去的秩序。

SOCIETY

PROGRESS

Those with higher Progress scores believe in social change and rationality. Though not always, they are usually secular or atheist, and support environmental action and scientific or technological research. 在这个轴得分高的人相信社会变革和理性。他们通常是世俗的或是无神论者，并支持环保行动，以及科技研发。


About This Project 关于本项目

8iValues is a fork of the 8values political quiz. The goal here is not for users to take the quiz, but to display the answers and political compass of various Large Language Models (LLMs). 8iValues 是基于 8values 政治测验的改版项目。本项目的目标不是让用户自行答题，而是展示不同大语言模型 (LLM) 对同一组政治价值观问题的回答和政治倾向分析。

Each AI model independently answered all questions and provided explanations for their answers. You can view the overall political leaning of each model, as well as their specific answers and reasoning. 每个 AI 模型独立回答了所有问题，并对每个回答提供了解释。您可以查看每个模型的总体政治倾向评分，以及它对每道题的具体回答和理由。

What's the "Closest Match" mean at the bottom of the results? 结果底部的“最近的匹配”是什么意思？

In addition to matching to the eight values, the quiz also attempts to match to a political ideology. This is a work in progress and is much less accurate than the values and axes, so don't take it too seriously. 除了计算在八大价值观的倾向上外，还会尝试匹配政治意识形态。然而，此项功能的准确度非常难以保证，远远不如直接看其在政治轴线上的位置，因此不要过于当真。
